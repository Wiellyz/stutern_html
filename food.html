Jollof Rice & Chicken

Jollof rice and chicken is stewed rice with finely spiced and fried chicken, it is most times seved at parties alongside dodo(plantain)

IT is a nigerian dish popularly known as party jollof, it is most times eaten on special occasions. It is a NIgerian favourite. Although different countries have their own version of the meal

Ingredients Measurements
Rice A cup of rice
Groundnut oil 1/2 a up of groundnut oil
Pepper and Tomatoes(grinded) 3 cups of grinded pepper and tomatoes
Onions # medium size onions
Salt and seasoning cubes Use accorfing to your taste or want
Chicken Half a kilo of chicken breast

How To Make Jollof Rice & Chicken

Spice, boil and fry your chicken. Perboil the rice, and wash it. Pour in the groundnut oil your pepper and tomatoes as well as your sliced and diced onions and let it fry. Add your salt and seasoning cubes, let it fry a little. Pour the perboiled rice, let it cook for about five minutes, then pour in your chicken and let it cook till the rice is done
